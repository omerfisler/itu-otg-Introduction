Menemen


Description

Menemen is a popular traditional Turkish dish that includes eggs, tomato, green peppers, and spices such as ground black and red pepper cooked in olive oil. You can find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 3 green peppers (after removing the stems and seeds, finely chopped)
  • 3 medium tomatoes
  • 1/2 (half) teaspoon of salt
  • 3 eggs
  • Optional: half a small bowl of cheddar cheese would be enough.

Steps

  1. Add oil and peppers to the pan and fry until the peppers change color.
  2. Add peeled and finely chopped tomatoes.
  3. Cover the pan on low heat and wait for the tomatoes to cook thoroughly.
  4. If the tomatoes are sticking to the pan, you can add a little boiling water. This usually won't be necessary.
  5. Once the tomatoes are cooked, you can break the eggs in, as shown in the photo. You can either whisk the eggs in a separate bowl and add them, or, like I did, break them into the pan and stir them in.
  6. Add salt and any spices you like and wait until the eggs are cooked.
  7. If you'd like to add cheddar cheese, you can do so at this stage. Serve the menemen hot.
