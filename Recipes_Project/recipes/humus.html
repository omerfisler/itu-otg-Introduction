Hummus


Description

Hummus a Levantine dip, spread, or savory dish made from cooked, mashed chickpeas blended with tahini, lemon juice, and garlic

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of boiled chickpeas
  • 1/3 cup tahini
  • Juice of 1 lemon or 2-3 tablespoons of ready-made lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 (half) teaspoon cumin
For the topping:
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Red pepper
  • Sumac

Steps

  1. First, let's peel the boiled chickpeas with our hands.
  2. Then, put the chickpeas in the food processor and add the lemon juice, tahini, garlic, cumin and salt and mix.
  3. Once all the ingredients are combined, open the food processor lid. If the hummus isn't the consistency you're looking for, adjust it by adding water.
  4. Let's put the hummus we prepared on a serving plate and shape it with a spoon.
  5. Then, to decorate, let's put boiled chickpeas in the middle and drizzle olive oil colored with red pepper.
  6. Finally, sprinkle with sumac and finely chopped parsley and serve. Hummus is a very easy appetizer to make, and bon appetit for everyone who tries it!
